## Subject
**Inquiry into Potential Doctrinal Deviation**  
Concerning **Lyosha Ohotnik** and associated lay preaching within Bard’s Gate.

---

## Mandate of Inquiry

By directive of the Synod, I traveled to Bard’s Gate to assess two matters:

1. **Whether Dawnfather [[Lyosha Ohotnik]]**, given the well-documented history of his lineage, has retained orthodoxy without regression or concealed deviation.

2. **Whether an unordained half-blood woman preaching under Pelor’s name in the Dregs Quarter** represents doctrinal corruption or false authority.

---

# I. On Dawnfather Lyosha Ohotnik

Given the historical record attached to the **Ohotnik name**, scrutiny is warranted.

I did not meet Lyosha Ohotnik directly, as he is presently located in the capital. However, I conducted the following investigations:

- Review of temple records and liturgical documentation  
- Examination of sermon transcripts  
- Witness testimony from clergy  
- Informal observation of adherents and disciples

### Findings

No evidence of:

- Proscribed study or concealed experimentation  
- Deviation in liturgical structure  
- “Ends justify the means” rhetoric  
- Private doctrine divergent from temple teaching

Those questioned described him as:

- **Firm**
- **Restrained**
- **Disciplined in matters of undeath and corruption**

Severity, where present, remains **within doctrinal bounds**.

There is **no evidence of retained influence** from the excesses historically associated with his lineage.

If he bears the weight of that history, he bears it **soberly**.

**Conclusion:**  
No corrective action indicated.

---

# II. On Druuna Sunseeker

The individual in question identifies herself as:

**[[Druuna Sunseeker]]**  
Disciple of Lyosha Ohotnik  
Believer in Pelor

She claims:

- No rank  
- No ordination  
- No ecclesiastical authority  

This statement is **accurate**.

---

## Doctrinal Competence

Druuna demonstrates **incomplete doctrinal retention**.

She fails precise recitation of formal:

- Tenets  
- Litanies  
- Structured catechism

Her memory for formal theology is weak.

However:

- She **does not claim authority she does not possess**
- She **does not solicit tithe or wealth**
- She **does not introduce novel doctrine**
- She **does not contradict established teaching**

Her ministry consists primarily of:

- Feeding the poor  
- Tending wounds  
- Discouraging retaliatory violence  
- Offering prayer without coercion

---

## Examination

When questioned directly under formal examination:

- Her **doctrinal precision faltered**.

Her **faith did not**.

She answered questions regarding:

- Mercy  
- Hope  
- Divine purpose  

without calculation or embellishment.

Her responses were **simple, unadorned, and consistent with orthodoxy in spirit**, if not in precise doctrinal phrasing.

She is **unsuited for theological dispute**.

She is **well suited for service**.

---

## Instruction Issued

To prevent doctrinal drift born of poor retention, I have instructed that **Druuna Sunseeker present monthly for continued instruction** at the nearest temple authority.

---

# III. Environmental Assessment

The **Dregs Quarter of Bard’s Gate** is volatile but **not doctrinally rebellious**.

Observations:

- Local half-blood residents exhibit **protective loyalty toward Druuna**.
- A defensive posture was observed during my arrival.
- Said posture dissipated upon confirmation of doctrinal orthodoxy.

No evidence was discovered of:

- Organized sectarian activity  
- Cultic deviation  
- Schismatic movement  

---

# Determination

**Lyosha Ohotnik** shows **no evidence of backsliding or concealed deviation**, despite the historical concerns attached to his lineage.

**Druuna Sunseeker:**

- Is **not a heretic**  
- Is **not a false prophet**  
- Is **not exploiting Pelor’s name**

She is an **imperfect lay disciple acting in good faith**.

**Recommendations**

- No sanction recommended  
- No censure recommended  
- Pastoral oversight sufficient

---

# Final Note

The rumor prompting this inquiry appears to have arisen from **prejudice regarding lineage**, rather than substantiated doctrinal concern.

> The Dawnfather’s light is not diminished by imperfect vessels.
