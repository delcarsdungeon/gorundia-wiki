# The King’s Chamber – After the Argument

The guard outside the chamber barely manages a nod before stepping aside.

“You’re expected.”

The doors open.

The party walks in on the **tail end of a sentence**.

Prince Porthos’s voice carries across the chamber, sharp and openly mocking.

“…perhaps instead you can give it to Dame Rykeena. She’s a younger version and would make a loyal baroness.”

The tone is loud.

Deliberately sarcastic.

The room is a wreck.

A small side table has been **smashed to splinters**.  
Broken cups lie scattered across the floor, water spreading across the stone like spilled rain.

[[King Rimmand II]] stands near the center of the chamber.

Fists clenched.  
Jaw tight.

He is very clearly on the edge of shouting at his son.

Across from him stands [[Porthos Ryomar]], chin lifted in defiance.

For a moment the king opens his mouth—

Then he notices the party standing in the doorway.

The room freezes.

The spell that kept the argument private had also muffled the door.

No one heard the knock.

To one side of the chamber, [[Vladek Orenic]] has his back to the party.

His wife stands beside him, one hand on his arm, clearly trying to calm him down.

Across the room sits [[Glandyr Ryomar]], scowling darkly.

A **fresh black eye** is already swelling beneath his brow.

[[Kylesstra Ravenster]] leans close to him, carefully prodding the bruise with two fingers.

“Hold still,” she murmurs calmly. “I’m checking if he broke anything.”

Near the far wall, [[Eldric Allaric]] is pacing slowly, hands clasped behind his back.

[[Maris Allaric]] stands nearby, watching the scene with a deep frown.

When the party enters, **Allaric is the first to regain his composure**.

He straightens.  
Smooths his coat.

The king manages to collect himself a moment later, though the anger still sits plainly in his face.

Only then does **Vladek Orenic finally turn**.

His nose is **bleeding**.

His expression is pure thunder.

He glowers at the newcomers for a long moment, wipes the blood away with the back of his hand, and walks quietly to his seat.

Behind him, the Duchess Orenic notices [[Sorek Redmarch]].

She gives him a quick, conspiratorial **wink**.

For a moment, the only sound in the chamber is the slow drip of water from the shattered cups onto the stone floor.

## The King’s Explanation

For a moment no one speaks.

The king draws a breath and forces his voice back into something resembling calm.

“Gentlemen.”

His eyes flick to the broken table.

Then to the dukes.

Then back to the party.

“We were discussing the **final disposition of the Bordella matter**, and the resolution of Baron DeSalle's cowardice.”

He gestures vaguely toward the room.

“As you can see, the discussion became… spirited.”

His gaze shifts briefly toward the dukes of Orenic and Ravenster before returning to the PCs.

“Duke Allaric quite reasonably wished to make clear that Baron DeSalle remains his vassal, and that matters concerning his barony should involve his counsel.”

A brief pause.

“Duke Ryomar and Duke Orenic disagreed rather loudly about the interpretation of recent events.”

That is as far as the king is willing to go.

He straightens slightly.

“The matter itself remains straightforward.”

“Lord Mayor Ponzio Bordella and his brother Allec will be dealt with according to the laws of the realm.”

He folds his hands behind his back.

“And that is what we are here to determine.”

Then, noticing the blood and swelling bruises around the chamber, the king snaps toward the door.

“Healer!”

His voice hits the **wall of silence** and dies there.

Rimmand’s jaw tightens as he realizes what he’s just done.

“Send for a healer,” he growls.

A beat.

“**Lyosha specifically.**”

Only then does he stride to the edge of the ward and lean his head out into the hallway, repeating the order beyond the spell’s reach before stepping back inside the chamber.

Behind him, **Sorek Redmarch** has quietly turned his head away, pretending to study the wall while the Duchess Orenic watches him with a faintly amused smile.

The room settles.

Barely.

And all eyes turn to **Beringer**.
