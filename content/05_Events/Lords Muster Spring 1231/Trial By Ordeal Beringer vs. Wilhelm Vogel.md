# Address of King Rimmand II — Morning of the Ordeal

_Dawn light spills pale across the eastern field beyond the capital walls. Nobles gather at measured distance. The sanctified ground waits. The King steps forward beneath the standard of Gorundia._

**King Rimmand II speaks.**

“Lords and Ladies of Gorundia… sworn knights… faithful citizens.”

“We gather this morning not in celebration… nor in conquest… but in judgment.”

_A measured pause._

“Two men stand before us.”

“Both sons of Galmyr Vogel.”

“Both sworn to the defense of this realm.”

“Both accused — in different ways — of ambition that may shape the fate of this kingdom.”

---

“Let it be known: this field is not convened for spectacle.”

“It is not convened for vengeance.”

“It is not convened for sport.”

**“It is convened for clarity.”**

“The Right of Ordeal is ancient. It predates this crown, these walls, and many of the houses represented here.”

“When words divide a realm beyond reconciliation… steel may render an answer where tongues cannot.”

---

“Hear me clearly.”

“The outcome of this duel shall not fracture Gorundia.”

“It shall not license private war.”

“It shall not justify vendetta.”

**“The judgment of this field will be accepted as law.”**

“If Duke Wilhelm Vogel prevails, his honor stands before the Crown.”

“If Sir Beringer Amadis prevails, his standing shall be recognized under the same law.”

“Neither man fights for a crown this morning.”

“They fight for legitimacy.”

---

_The King’s voice lowers, but carries._

“Blood has been named.”

“Allegiance has been questioned.”

“Hell itself has been invoked.”

“Let Heaven now bear witness.”

---

“By the authority of the Crown… by the rites of the Triad… by the sanctification of this ground…”

**“Let the Ordeal proceed.”**
