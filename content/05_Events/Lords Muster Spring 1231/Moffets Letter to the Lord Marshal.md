> [!important] Quinn Moffet — Letter of Recognition to the Lord Marshal
> **To Sir Beringer Amadis**  
> *Lord Marshal of Gorundia*  
> *Warden of the King’s Peace*  
> *Bearer of the Crown’s Commission*
>
> By the seal and word of **House Moffet**, let this letter stand as a matter of record.
>
> Be it known that **House Moffet** hereby recognizes the lawful appointment of **Sir Beringer Amadis** as **Lord Marshal of Gorundia**, acting by the authority of the Crown and within the bounds of established law and custom.
>
> House Moffet affirms its respect for the office of the Lord Marshal, and declares its intent to cooperate fully and in good faith with all lawful commands, inquiries, and duties issued under that authority, insofar as they pertain to the keeping of the King’s Peace and the defense of the Realm.
>
> This declaration is offered publicly and without reservation, as an acknowledgment of lawful office and jurisdiction. It is **not tendered as an oath**, nor as a binding covenant of arms, but as a statement of standing, recognition, and willingness to act in accordance with the law as it is written and applied.
>
> Let it further be recorded that **House Moffet makes this declaration freely**, without compulsion or duress, and with full knowledge of its import.
>
> May the law be served with clarity.  
> May justice be rendered without fear or favor.  
> And may those who hold authority be judged by their works.
>
> **Issued under my hand,**  
> in witness of all who see and hear,
>
> **Quinn Moffet**  
> *Heir of House Moffet*  
> *In the name and standing of the House*
>
> *[Seal of House Moffet affixed in red wax]*
