## The Orenic–Ravenskär Entanglement

_Blood ties, bad decisions, and a friendship that keeps heralds employed._

---

### Family, Technically

**Duke Vladek Orenic** gestures vaguely between **Elspeth** and himself, as though tracing a line only he finds important.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“You know, of course, zat your mother is Orenic.”

Elspeth stiffens slightly.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“Cousins, once removed. Ravenster by marriage, but Orenic in the blood.”

He looks at her with fond exasperation.

**Duke Vladek (in accented Gorundian, using the wrong term deliberately):**  
“Ah, _dochenka_… niece.”

**[GM NOTE]** This is not technically correct. He knows this. He does not care.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“Your mother was always too free in her choices.”

He wags a finger at Elspeth, stern but smiling.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“Running off from Orenic was bad judgment.”

A beat.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“But marrying Glandyr Ryomar?”

He sighs heavily.

**Duke Vladek:** “Zat was _inspired_ madness.”

---

### The Tournament That Started It

The Duke’s expression shifts—nostalgic now, amused.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“First time I met your father, we were young and stupid.”  
“He wore too much gold. I wore too much confidence.”

He chuckles.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“Tournament. Melee round.”

He makes a broad sweeping gesture.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“We knock each other flat.”  
“Then we stand up.”  
“Zen we knock each other flat again.”

He spreads his hands.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“Heralds panic. Courtiers panic.”  
“Someone shouts ‘honor.’ Someone else shouts ‘stop.’”

He grins.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“We ignore zem and start wrestling.”

A beat.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“Eventually it turns into laughter.”

**Duke Vladek:**  
“Zen drinks.”

He nods, satisfied.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“Heralds have never forgiven us.”

---

### Modern Consequences

The Duke shrugs.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“Now we are both important men.”  
“High peers.”  
“Zis makes it worse.”

He chuckles darkly.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“Any time we meet, half the court prepares apologies and the other half prepares bets.”

He glances at Elspeth again.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“So yes, _dochenka_.”  
“I scold you.”

His tone softens.

**Duke Vladek:**  
“But you come by it honestly.”

Witchholme creaks, approving of generational chaos.

### Drop-In Uses

- Whenever Glandyr and Vladek are in the same room
- As court gossip explaining bruised heralds
- To justify Vladek’s familiarity with Elspeth
- As a warning sign before a “friendly” duel breaks out
