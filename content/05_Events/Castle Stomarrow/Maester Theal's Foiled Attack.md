## Aftermath at Stormarrow Keep

[[Braga Kharne]], [[Orin Kharne]], [[Kat Kendal]]

Still stunned, Braga starts to talk when Orin sits next to her.
![[Orin offers comfort.png|340]]
**Braga:** “A man died for me tonight. I didn’t even know his name, but I’m a baroness now, so his death’s on my banner whether I like it or not. He saved my life with his courage—once I had my axe, I finished off that… thing.”

_She means the raven knight._

**Braga:** “And then Dana came in like a storm with Blue at her back.

Heh. Took three of us. Three strong women—stronger than some men, but…

Standing over that dead... hero when it’s done – I know you... you can walk away knowing you would’ve done the same, could’ve done the same...  
  
I can’t.  
  
I keep thinking about his mother. His sister. His wife—if he had one. Faces I’ve never seen but can’t stop seeing.”

She meets Orin’s eyes.

**Braga:** “You know why there are so few women knights? I do. Venom told me once—half-joking, but she was right. Plenty of elven women master the bow, some take to the spear, and a rare few master the sword. But it’s different when the killing’s up close. Men seem better at shutting it out. Women… we feel it. We see them. And that’s harder to live with when you’re standing close enough to look them in the eye.”

She glances around to make sure no one else is in earshot, then leans in slightly.

**Braga:** “And yeah… you’re better at it than I am.”

A wry smirk tugs at her mouth.

**Braga:** “Don’t you dare laugh, or I’ll make you prove it in the yard tomorrow.”

Her face hardens.

**Braga:** “And then there was the prisoner. He came to kill Lyosha. _Lyosha_. A man who’s given everything to heal others. A man who still gets spit on for his name.”

She goes cold at that.

**Braga:** “He saved Venom. He’s patched you all up time and again. Beringer asked me what to do with the prisoner—maybe testing me, maybe punishing me. I pronounced judgment and meted out justice. I don’t like it, and I don’t regret it. I just… don’t know how you do it sometimes.”

She leans in and hugs Orin around the waist, and that’s when Kat Kendal steps in, just inside the door.

**Kat:** “Baron. Baroness. The main hall’s ready—if you’d like to join us.”

_Kat says it polite and bright._

As Kat turns away, Braga watches her go with a mix of envy and sadness.

**Braga:** “It’s still exciting for her. She did well tonight, I’m told. But you know what? She’s going to cry, a lot. I don’t know when, but she will.”

Braga shakes her head.

**Braga:** “Blue’s the only honest one about it.”

She sighs, muttering under her breath:

**Braga:** “I look like hell, feel worse… but BY THE GAWDS, I’ve got my axe right here, so nobody better call it out.”

She tries to smile up at Orin, but it’s the kind of smile that says _I’ll keep moving—because I have to._
