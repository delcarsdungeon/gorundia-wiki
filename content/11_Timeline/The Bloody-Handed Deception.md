Following the destruction of the Hextorite worshipers at their Fortress-Monastery, and the parties attack on the Yuan-Ti, the Archon of [[Erythnul the Many]] disguised himself as [[Orin Kharne]] and killed [[Elyndra Sylvanranth]] in a bloody murder as retaliation.  

The [[Iron Wolves]] were on his tail, and a 500 gold bounty placed upon his head.

It was not long before Orin cleared his name, but the "Bloody-Handed" moniker stuck, to his chagrin.
