# Campaign Timeline

A chronological record of major events in the campaign.

---

## 1230

### Early Summer

- [[Slaying of the Tumor Beast]] — The party’s first adventure, destroying the monstrous creature lurking beneath the caves.

### Mid Summer

- [[The Siege of Castle Ringhead]] — The party escorts [[Hana Shineapple]] to the castle and becomes trapped during a goblin siege.

### Late Summer

- [[The Bloody-Handed Deception]] — A false [[Orin Kharne]] murders [[Elyndra Sylvanranth]], damaging Orin’s reputation and deepening political tensions.

### Brewfest Week  
*(Festival period between Harvester and Patchwall)*

- [[The Brewfest Tournament]] — A week-long festival tournament drawing knights and adventurers from across the realm.  The King arrived at [[Bard's Gate]] eager to show a keen interest in the frontier.
