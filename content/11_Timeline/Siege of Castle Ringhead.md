The party was set upon, and sieged by a goblin army while exploring [[Castle Ringhead]].  They have rescued and are escorting [[Hana Shineapple]], unfortunately her hired ranger companion fell to the beast in the halls.

[[Ouro Ohotnik]] displayed his terrible power, the moat of tar aflame, he forced the goblin and hobgoblin warriors to march into the inferno to a painful death.
