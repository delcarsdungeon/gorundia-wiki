# Dwarvish Laws Governing Clan Formation

## The Law of Ancestry
**Requirement:**  
The founder must be able to trace their lineage back to a recognized Dwarvish ancestor.

---

## The Law of Honor
**Requirement:**  
The prospective clan must have no blemishes upon its honor, such as criminal activity, oath-breaking, or betrayal.

> [!tip] Plot Hook
> Rival clans may attempt to sabotage the founding by falsely accusing the new clan of dishonorable acts.

---

## The Law of Wealth
**Requirement:**  
The new clan must possess sufficient wealth to support its members and contribute to the prosperity of the Dwarvish kingdom.

> [!tip] Plot Hook
> The players may need to seek a hidden treasure, secure a patron, or complete a lucrative contract to gather the required funds.

---

## The Law of Skill
**Requirement:**  
The clan must demonstrate mastery in a particular craft, trade, or art worthy of dwarven recognition.

> [!tip] Plot Hook
> The players may need to master a rare skill or create a masterpiece to prove their worth before the clan elders.

---

# Ceremonial Details and Traditions

## Ancestral Hall
A newly founded clan must establish an **Ancestral Hall**, where a hearth fire is lit using flame drawn from the sacred fire of the mountain.

## Clan Totem
A totem representing the clan’s spirit, symbol, or ancestral animal must be carved and formally blessed by the elders.

## Stone Tablet
A stone tablet bearing the clan’s **name, symbol, and founding date** must be carved and placed within the **Hall of Records**, ensuring the clan’s recognition in the annals of dwarven history.
