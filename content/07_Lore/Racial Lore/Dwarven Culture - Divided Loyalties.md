## Divided Loyalties

Because a dwarf is expected to be loyal to **his household, his guild, his caste, his clan, and his vault**, every dwarf faces the possibility of divided loyalties and conflicting duties. Not every dwarf manages to navigate these complexities successfully.

The archetypical dwarven tragedy, **_Fall of the House of Hurgon_**, features a Highborn hero forced to choose between his **vault lord, clan chief, and household**.

After a series of poor choices in the first act:

- the **vault of Hurgon** is destroyed  
- his **clan dissolves**  
- his **family is massacred**

In the second act, Hurgon carves **oaths of vengeance into his own flesh**. The third act follows his red-handed campaign of revenge before he finally **immolates himself in the forges of Azen Khador**.

Hurgon is revered as **the First Fury**, but his tragedy remains a cautionary tale among the dwarves.
