# Shineapple Field Report – Druuna Sunseeker

> [!important] Summary
> A written field report from **Erich Shineapple** to **Dawnfather [[Lyosha Ohotnik]]** regarding the activities and reception of [[Druuna Sunseeker]] among the people of **[[The Dregs]]**, prior to her formal examination by a paladin of the faith.

---

## Letter Carried by [[Guaco]]

**From:** Erich Shineapple  
**To:** Dawnfather [[Lyosha Ohotnik]]

---

Dawnfather,

You asked how the Dreggs hold.

They hold.

Violence is lower than it was during the [[Jackal Killings]]. Not gone. Just quieter. People are cautious now. Watching.

Your priestess remains active.

[[Druuna Sunseeker]].

Initial reaction to her pacifism was mockery. Testing. Physical correction.

She did not respond with force.

She did not argue.

She did not retreat permanently.

She returns after each incident. Healed. Robes stained. Mud at the hem. Blood not fully scrubbed from stitching.

She does not reference it.

She resumes speaking.

Pattern repeated multiple times.

Community perception has shifted.

At first she was considered soft. Now she is considered stubborn.

Three laborers now stand within arm’s reach during gatherings. Two youths escort her at dusk.

She has not requested protection.

Retaliation has occurred against at least one aggressor. She was not present. No indication she ordered it. She did not inquire publicly.

Community does not adopt her pacifism.

They adopt her resilience.

Her faith presentation is consistent. No theatrics. No condemnation. Emphasis on endurance and mercy.

She has mastered basic clerical workings. Healing is competent. Minor light and blessing effects are reliable. No advanced miracles observed.

She is not strategic.

She is persistent.

If escalation occurs beyond fists, she may not survive without intervention.

She will not ask.

That is all.

— **Erich Shineapple**

---

## Context

This report was delivered to **[[Lyosha Ohotnik]]** shortly before the **trial of faith arranged for [[Druuna Sunseeker]]** by the Pelorite order.
