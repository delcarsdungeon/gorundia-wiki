# **Vharak-Thul, the Rot That Walks**

*The Green That Lies*

> *“Greens don’t kill you.*
> *They make sure you were already dead when they get there.”*
> — **Sorek Redmarch**

---

## **Overview**

Vharak-Thul was an ancient green dragon responsible for the devastation of multiple Grugach clans and the near-collapse of entire forest regions.

Unlike red dragons, which destroy openly, Vharak-Thul operated through:

> **Corruption, deception, and slow ecological collapse.**

Its death—won by Serelith Bloodwulf and her allies—came at catastrophic cost, leaving the Bloodwulf line shattered and the land itself scarred.

---
## **Observed (Reliable Record)**

* Identified in Grugach oral record as:

  * **“The Rot That Walks”**
  * **“The Green That Lies”** 

---
### **Environmental Effects**

* Long-term poisoning of:

  * Water sources
  * Game populations
  * Forest stability

> “Leaves rotted while still green… water tasted wrong… the wind lied.” 

---
### **Behavioral Traits**

* Extensive shapeshifting:

  * Elf
  * Human
  * Child

* Psychological manipulation:

  * Deception
  * Luring
  * Misdirection

---
### **Druidic Intervention (Critical Addition)**

During the engagement:

* Druidic circles (identified in oral accounts as **“the horned ones”**) entered the contaminated zones

* Their actions:

  * **Absorbed and resisted the spreading corruption**
  * **Blunted the dragon’s breath effects**
  * Stabilized:

    * soil
    * water
    * immediate terrain

> They did not cleanse it.
>
> They made it **survivable**.

---

### **Cost**

* Multiple druidic circles were lost

> “Three clans gone.”
> (Grugach) 

Correlated with:

* “Circles fell” (cross-source inference)
