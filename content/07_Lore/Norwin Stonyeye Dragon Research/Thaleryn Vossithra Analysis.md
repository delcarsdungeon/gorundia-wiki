# **Thaleryn Vossithra**

*The Sister Beneath the Roots*

> *“Everyone’s got a plan for dragons.*
> *Kill it. Drive it off. Pray it leaves.*
>
> *No one’s got a plan for the one that just… stays under your feet.”*
> — **Sorek Redmarch**

---

## **Overview**

Thaleryn Vossithra is an ancient green dragon, identified through wood elf funerary record as a counterpart to [[Vharak-Thul]] and a member of the same draconic grouping as [[Zara Vallytrex]].

She was not slain.

She was not driven off.

She remains.

But not as she was.

---

## **Observed (Historical Record)**

* Named in wood elf dirge as:

  * **Thaleryn Vossithra** 

---

### **Physical State**

* Sustained catastrophic injury:

> *“Her wings did not carry her again.”* 

* Result:

  * Permanent loss of flight
  * Forced transition to ground and subsurface movement

---

### **Behavioral Shift**

> *“She went to ground… not to hide. To remain.”* 

* Abandoned aerial dominance
* Adopted **subterranean domain**

---

### **Movement Pattern**

> *“Not above us. Through us.”* 

* Operates:

  * beneath root systems
  * within soil and deep growth

---

### **Engagement Outcome**

* Driven into deep-root regions
* Not killed
* Not pursued further

---

### **Final Recorded State**

> *“She listens where the roots are thickest.”* 

---

## **Current Intelligence (Post-Conflict Findings)**

*(Confidence: Moderate to High — multiple corroborating indicators)*

---

### **Location & Condition**

* Thaleryn is believed to be:

> **Embedded beneath a sacred wood elf tree system**


---

### **State**

* Semi-dormant
* **Chemically or magically suppressed**

Described as:

> **“drugged” rather than bound**

---

### **Primary Activity**

* Continuous feeding behavior:

> **Consumption of deep root systems and sap flow**

---

### **Critical Function (New Discovery)**

Her feeding is not incidental.

It is being **used**.

---

### **Sap Processing Mechanism**

* The sacred tree’s sap:

  * passes through her system
  * is altered during digestion/processing

* Resulting byproduct is:

> **Essential to the production of cloned goblins**

---

### **Implication**

She is not merely contained.

She is:

> **Being exploited as a biological-alchemical processor**

---
