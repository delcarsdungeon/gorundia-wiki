> [!quote]

## **Overview**

Zarathryss Vaelorath—known publicly as **Zara Vallytrex**—is an ancient bronze dragon and the sole confirmed surviving member of the draconic grouping now referred to as **The Green Triad**.

Where her counterparts were:

* Slain → [[Vharak-Thul]]
* Driven below and altered → [[Thaleryn Vossithra]]

Zara:

> **Survived. Fled. Changed. Returned.**

She is no longer acting as a simple territorial or ecological entity.

She is:

> **A political actor operating under constraint.**
