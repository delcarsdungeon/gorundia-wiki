# **Voice of the Grugach — The Rot War**

*Composite oral account, recorded by Norwin Stonyeye*

---

> *“Do not make it sound better than it was.”*
> *“Say it straight.”*

---

They do not begin with the dragon.

They begin with what was lost.

---

> “Three clans gone.”
> “Not broken.”
> “Gone.”

> “No fires left.”
> “No names carried.”

---

> “Leaves rotted while still green.”
> “Game fled.”
> “Water tasted wrong.”

> “The wind lied.”

---

Name follows.

Spoken the same way by every voice.

---

> “**Vharak-Thul.**”

> “The Rot That Walks.”
> “The Green That Lies.”

---

> “He did not hunt to eat.”

> “He hunted to empty the land.”

---

### **On the Coming of Death**

---

> “He breathed the green death.”

> “Not smoke.”
> “Not flame.”

> “Breath that stays.”

---

> “You do not run from it.”

> “It waits.”

---

> “It takes the ground.”
> “Then it takes you.”

---

### **On the Horned Ones**

*(tone changes here—slower, deliberate, no exaggeration)*

---

> “Then the horned ones came.”

---

> “Not for glory.”
> “Not for song.”

---

> “They walked into the breath.”

---

> “We told them not to.”

> “They did not listen.”

---

> “They put hands to earth.”

> “To water.”
> “To the rot.”

---

> “And they held it.”

---

> “Not clean.”
> “Never clean.”

---

> “Held.”

---

> “Where they stood—
> the green death stopped moving.”

---

> “It still killed.”

> “But it did not spread.”

---

A pause here in all versions.

Not grief.

Recognition.

---

> “They died standing.”

---

> “No blades in them.”
> “No teeth in them.”

---

> “The rot took them.”

---

> “They did not step back.”

---

> “We remember that.”

---

### **On Outsiders**

---

> “Knights came.”
> “Iron and banners.”

> “Brave.”
> “Dead.”

---

> “Elves came.”
> “Songs and silver.”

> “Better.”
> “Still dead.”

---

> “We died knowing.”

---

### **On Theron Sylvanranth**

---

> “There was one.”

> “Looked like a man.”
> “Was not.”

---

> “Too still.”
> “Too sharp.”

> “Eyes like he was always choosing where to kill.”

---

> “He did not sing.”
> “He did not boast.”

> “He watched.”

> “Then he moved.”

---

> “Not soft like elves.”
> “Not loud like men.”

> “Wrong mix.”

> “Good for killing.”

---

> “He stood.”

> “When others pulled back—he stood.”

> “When others died—he stepped forward.”

---

> “The dragon took his arm.”

---

> “Not in one bite.”
> “Slow.”

---

> “He did not scream.”

---

### **On Faelor Stormarrow**

---

> “And the bow-master.”

> “**Faelor Stormarrow.**”

---

> “Not born to us.”
> “Chosen.”

---

> “His arrows did not miss.”

> “Not sky.”
> “Not scale.”
> “Not the places between breaths.”

---

> “When the dragon took the air—he bled it.”

> “When it hid in green—he found it.”

---

> “He did not waste arrows.”

> “Each one mattered.”
> “Each one hurt.”

---

> “They fought like brothers.”

> “Not same blood.”
> “Same hunt.”

---

### **The Slaughter**

---

> “He poisoned valleys and watched.”
> “He broke herds and followed fear.”

---

> “He wore shapes.”

> “Elf.”
> “Man.”
> “Child.”

> “Always smiling.”

---

> “We learned too late.”

---

> “Fire does not kill rot.”
> “Steel does not kill rot.”

---

> “Only hunger.”
> “Only pain.”

---

> “We drove him.”

> “No rest.”
> “No sleep.”

---

> “Break the shape.”
> “Bleed the sky.”

---

> “Three days.”
> “Three nights.”

> “No rites.”
> “No songs.”

> “No dead buried.”

---

### **The Kill**

---

> “Serelith Bloodwulf took the throat.”

> “Not clean.”
> “Nothing about him was clean.”

---

> “The bow-master opened him.”

> “The one-armed man held him.”

---

### **Final Words of Vharak-Thul**

---

> “He laughed.”

> “Said we had not won.”

> “Said the forest would remember him.”

> “Said his sister still listened beneath the roots.”

---

Silence follows.

Every time.

---

### **Aftermath**

---

> “We burned what we could.”

> “We buried what we could not.”

> “Some we left.”

---

> “We watched the ground.”

> “A season.”
> “And a day.”

---

> “The forest lived.”

---

> “We did not.”

---

## **Norwin’s Notes (Appended)**

* Confirms **druidic intervention during poison phase**

  * Not cleansing
  * **Containment under extreme cost**

* Grugach classification of druids:

  * Not priests
  * Not mages

> **Necessary.**

---

### **Margin Addendum**

> *They do not praise the druids.*
>
> *They do not mourn them the way they mourn their own.*
>
> They do something worse.
>
> *They treat what they did as expected.*
>
> Which means:
>
> *If the druids had not stood in that poison—*
>
> *no one else would have.*
