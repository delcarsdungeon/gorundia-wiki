# Recovered Document  
## The Assassination Plan

*Fragmentary notes describing the intended assassination of King Priam Ryomar II.*

---

## Original Operational Design

The intended operation relied on a layered ritual structure designed to produce a **clean, untraceable royal death** during the Lord's Muster.

Key elements of the plan included:

1. **Removal of the Krozen** twenty-four hours before the gathering through infernal banishment.
2. Preparation of a **bloodline ritual** exploiting the shared ancestry of the Ryomar and Vogel lines.
3. Construction of a **name-binding curse** keyed to the King’s true-name fragments.
4. Routing the lethal effect **through the blood of [[Beringer]]**, whose lineage ties the Vogel and royal lines together.

The design would allow the curse to **travel along the bloodline connection**, striking the King while leaving magical traces implicating Beringer as the source.

## Intended Outcome

The King would die **without wound or spell signature**, appearing to have been slain through bloodline sorcery.

The resulting investigation would inevitably discover:

- [[Beringer]]’s Vogel ancestry
- lingering curse traces in his bloodline
- evidence suggesting he had unknowingly carried the killing magic

The conspirators expected the kingdom to conclude:

> The Lord Marshal himself had become the instrument of regicide.

---

## Additional Preparations

Other preparations referenced in the document include:

- Magical surveillance grids placed throughout the hall
- Summoning contingencies hidden in architectural fixtures
- Infernal reinforcements held in reserve
- Physical bodyguards positioned near the throne
- Political framing prepared in advance

The goal was simple:

> Kill the King **without a visible attack**,  
> frame [[Beringer]] for the crime,  
> and seize control of the resulting crisis.

---

## Observed Failures

According to later notes appended to the document:

- Surveillance systems were disabled
- Infernal reinforcements were destroyed
- Ritual timing collapsed
- The assassination sequence became unstable

Multiple lines in the margin simply read:

> “THEY RUINED THE PLAN.”
