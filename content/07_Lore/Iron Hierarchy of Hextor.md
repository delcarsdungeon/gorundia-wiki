# Iron Hierarchy of Hextor

The Church of [[Hextor]] is not merely a religion but a rigid military hierarchy.  
Its priesthood functions as both a spiritual authority and a command structure embedded within the armies and political institutions of the [[Dominion]].

The faithful refer to this structure as **The Iron Hierarchy**, reflecting Hextor’s doctrine that order is maintained through strength, fear, and absolute obedience.

Clergy frequently serve alongside Dominion military forces as **Commissars of the Iron Tyrant**, ensuring ideological loyalty and enforcing discipline through intimidation and execution when necessary.

---

# Supreme Authority

**Tyrant-Pontifex of the Scourge**

The supreme voice of Hextor in the mortal world.  
The Tyrant-Pontifex defines doctrine, proclaims crusades, appoints senior clergy, and holds absolute authority over the church’s militant orders.

---

# Grand Command

**Grand Legate of the Iron Tyranny**

Regional commander of Hextor’s church across large territories.

Grand Legates oversee:

- major temples and fortress-monasteries  
- large cult networks  
- crusading armies operating in Hextor’s name

---

# High Command

**Legate-Patriarch**

Senior war-priest responsible for ruling a major temple-fortress, monastery, or strategic region.

Legate-Patriarchs command both clergy and soldiers and frequently oversee covert cult operations beyond the Dominion’s borders.

*The monastery destroyed by the party during [[The Fall of Hextor’s Monastery]] was ruled by a Legate-Patriarch.*

---

**Iron Prelate**

Senior doctrinal authority tasked with enforcing orthodoxy and political loyalty.

Iron Prelates often serve as:

- inquisitors  
- interrogators  
- internal security officers within the church

---

# Field Command

**War Canon**

Battlefield priest who accompanies armies and leads soldiers in battle.

War Canons conduct battlefield rites and inspire troops with sermons glorifying conquest and domination.

---

**Fist-Captain of Hextor**

Knight-commander responsible for elite soldiers or temple guards.

Fist-Captains commonly lead:

- temple garrisons  
- heavy infantry companies  
- paladin detachments

---

# Commissariat

**Scourge Commissar**

Clerical officers attached directly to Dominion military units.

Their duties include:

- enforcing ideological loyalty  
- punishing cowardice or dissent  
- reporting disloyal officers  
- carrying out executions when discipline fails

Scourge Commissars are widely feared, as they possess authority over both soldiers and commanders.

Most are drawn from the ranks of **War Canons** or **Iron Prelates**.

---

# Officer Corps

**Iron Sergeant**

Veteran soldiers responsible for maintaining discipline among the rank and file.

Iron Sergeants serve as the brutal enforcers of Hextorite order.

---

**Temple Enforcer**

Professional soldiers sworn to the service of Hextor’s church.

They form the standard heavy infantry used to defend temples and enforce the church’s authority.

---

# Rank and File

**Chainbound Soldier**

Indentured or coerced soldiers forced into service through oath, debt, or punishment.

Many are criminals, conquered peoples, or fanatics seeking favor through violence.

---

# Lowest Rank

**Slave-Soldier (“The Shackled”)**

Expendable troops used for dangerous assaults, siege labor, and sacrificial charges.

Some units are literally chained together to prevent desertion.

---

# Doctrine of the Iron Hierarchy

The church teaches that hierarchy itself is sacred.

Strength justifies authority, and obedience is the highest virtue.  
Mercy is considered weakness, and dissent is treated as treason against both faith and state.

To Hextor’s faithful, the Iron Hierarchy represents the natural order of the world:  
the strong command, the weak obey, and the disobedient are destroyed.
