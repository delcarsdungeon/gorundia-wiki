
>[!Important] **By the Grace of the Archpaladin,**
>**Belvor the Fourth, King of Furyondy, Defender of the Faith, Lord of the Hart,**  to **His Majesty Rimmand the Second, King of Gorundia, Guardian of the Western Marches,** King of the Oeridian Homeland, greetings and honor in the sight of **Heironeous, the Archpaladin of Valor.**
>
>Word has reached our court in Chendl of your rising realm upon the eastern shores and of the steadfast devotion shown there to the Archpaladin whose banner we both bear. Though seas and mountains lie between our thrones, the bond of the **Invincible One** is stronger than distance, and it is meet that those who serve His cause know one another as brothers in arms and in faith.
>
>It is told to us that your lands lie near those ancient regions from which many of our forefathers once rode westward in elder days. Whether by chance or by the guiding hand of the Archpaladin Himself, it seems fitting that our peoples should now look upon one another not as strangers but as kin long parted by the turns of history.
>
>You will therefore receive at your court **Countess Neltara Blackheart**, whom we recognize as the duly appointed envoy and ambassador between our realms. She bears our trust and goodwill, and whatever courtesies and honors you extend to her shall be taken as courtesies extended to our crown.
>
>We are further informed that **the Court of Celene** has also taken notice of your kingdom and has seen fit to send its own envoys. This too we regard as a promising sign. The wise among us understand that the peace and stability of the Flanaess have ever depended upon the friendship of good and honorable realms, and the presence of both elven and human voices in such dialogue bodes well for the future.
>
>Should the will of the Archpaladin favor it, we would see the beginnings of friendship between Furyondy and Gorundia grow into fellowship in trade, diplomacy, and the mutual defense of all that is just and lawful in this troubled world.
>
>May the **Mighty Hand of Heironeous** guide your judgment and strengthen your arm.
>
>Given under our seal in the Royal City of **Chendl**,  
>in the **six hundred and first year of the Common Year**,  
>in faith and good will.
>
>**Belvor IV**  
>King of Furyondy  
>Defender of the Faith  
>Bearer of the Hart Crown

>[!note] **A Private Word from the Hand of Belvor IV**  
for **His Majesty Rimmand II of Gorundia**
> 
> Your Majesty,
> 
> The formal letter that accompanies this note bears the proper courtesies of crown and court. I ask your leave to speak here more plainly.
> 
> News has recently reached my court that **envoys of Celene have made contact with your kingdom**. That alone was enough to give me pause. The elves of that realm do not lightly extend their attention beyond their forests, nor do they spend their effort on lands they consider of little consequence.
> 
> When I learned further that your kingdom is said to honor **Heironeous**, the matter ceased to be merely curious and became worthy of immediate reply.
> 
> Thus you now receive **Countess Neltara Blackheart**, who bears my goodwill and my interest in better understanding the realm you rule. Her husband, **Delcar**, has spoken persuasively of the wisdom of opening dialogue between our kingdoms, and I find myself inclined to agree with him. In matters of diplomacy it is better for honorable realms to meet as friends before circumstances compel them to meet as strangers.
> 
> The reports that reach us suggest that Gorundia stands in lands far beyond those commonly traveled by the courts of the Flanaess. If this is so, then the discovery of a kingdom there—one that reveres the **Archpaladin** as we do—is a matter of no small importance.
> 
> Should the accounts prove true, it would please me greatly to know that the cause of the **Invincible One** has faithful champions in such distant lands.
> 
> I therefore welcome the opportunity for our courts to become acquainted.
> 
> Treat the Countess with the courtesy owed to a royal envoy, and know that whatever kindness you extend to her will be received here as kindness shown to my crown.
> 
> May the **Thunder of Heironeous** guide your judgment.
> 
> In good faith,
> 
> **Belvor IV**  
> King of Furyondy  
> Defender of the Faith


>[!important] **By the Grace and Thunder of Heironeous,**
>**Rimmand II, King of Gorundia, Warden of the Western Shores, Servant of the Archpaladin**,  
>to **Belvor the Fourth, King of Furyondy, Defender of the Faith and Lord of the Hart**,  greetings in honor and fellowship beneath the banner of the **Invincible One**.
>
>Your letter has been received with great satisfaction in our court.
>
>Though the distances between our realms are vast and the Sea of Dust lies between them like a scar upon the world, it is no small joy to learn that a mighty kingdom stands far to the north whose king serves the **Archpaladin** with the same devotion that guides our own hand.
>
>Such tidings are rare in these troubled ages.
>
>That the court of **Celene** has taken notice of Gorundia was already a matter of great interest among our scholars and counselors. That a **king devoted to Heironeous** should also reach forth his hand in friendship is a greater blessing still.
>
>Know therefore that your ambassador, **Countess Neltara Blackheart**, has been received with full honor appropriate to her station and to the trust you have placed in her.
>
>It is my hope that the goodwill expressed in your words may grow into true fellowship between our kingdoms. Though our lands lie far apart, those who kneel before the same altar are never truly strangers.
>
>To that end, I have instructed the priests of the Archpaladin within my court to begin preparations for the establishment of a **chain of sacred communication** between our realms, if the same may be arranged among the temples of your kingdom. Through prayer and the blessings granted by the Invincible One, distance may be shortened and the voices of faithful rulers carried where ships and riders cannot easily travel.
>
>If your own clergy find such a thing agreeable, our priests stand ready to cooperate in this holy work.
>
>May the **Mighty Hand of Heironeous** guide your rule and strengthen your sword arm in defense of justice.
>
>Given under our seal in the Royal City of **Karethlun**,  
>In the Year **1231 of the Oeridian Reckoning**.
>In honor and fellowship,
>**Rimmand II**  
>King of Gorundia  
>Paladin of Heironeous  
>Servant of the Archpaladin
