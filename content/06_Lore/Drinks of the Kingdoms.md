# Tavern Drinks of Bard’s Gate
*A curated list of wines, ales, and spirits found across the city.*

---

## 🍷 Elven Wines

> [!info] Rare Imports
> These wines are rarely found outside elven courts and wealthy noble tables. Most taverns only acquire them through special trade caravans or noble patronage.

### **Eldarion's Nectar**
**Price:** $1000 (≈ 2g 5s)

A luminous golden wine with rich notes of honey and elderflower. The finish is warm and lingering, leaving the faint impression of summer sunlight.  
Legends claim it was once served only at the tables of elven royalty.

---

### **Moonleaf Elixir**
**Price:** $500 (≈ 1g 2s)

A pale silvery wine brewed from moonberry and crushed silverleaf. Its flavor is soft, cool, and strangely calming.  
Some drinkers report feeling unusually serene—or unusually honest—after a glass.

---

### **Sylvan Blossom**
**Price:** $100 (≈ 5s)

A delicate floral wine carrying hints of jasmine, spring blossoms, and fresh forest air.  
Light-bodied and fragrant, it is often described as *“drinking the memory of an elven glade.”*

---
### **Troll Discharge**
**Price:** $20 (≈ 1s)

A violently green liquor of uncertain origin served in thick clay mugs. The flavor has been described as “medicinal,” “fermented moss,” and “a mistake best made quickly.”

Laborers claim it was first brewed by dwarves attempting to create a troll-resistant disinfectant. The experiment failed.

The drink did not.

> [!quote] Laborer's Wisdom
> “It’ll either cure what’s wrong with you… or make you forget you had it.”
