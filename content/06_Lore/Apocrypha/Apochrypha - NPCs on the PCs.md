# Apochrophal - NPCs on the PCs

Apocrypha

The following account is **not considered canonical history**.  
It represents a rumored or imagined meeting between several enemies of the realm — a reconstruction of how their ambitions and grievances might align.

Whether such a meeting ever truly occurred is unknown.

> *“Sometimes we get together behind the scenes and bullshit.”*  
> — Sorek Redmarch.

---
_A table littered with cups. Smoke curls through lamplight._

_Braga’s cigar burns slow._  
_Sorek’s flask clinks softly._  
_Venom sits straight, watchful._  
_Blue leans forward, hands fidgeting._  
_Theron sits in the shadow, words measured like a scalpel._

_The name **[[Thalrik]]** comes up often — too slippery a dwarf for anyone to quite pin down._

---

**Braga:**  
“[[Orin Kharne|Orin]]’s still out there swinging like he means to split the world in half. I love him for it, but he’ll break himself before he bends. [[Wilhelm Vogel|Vogel]]’s counting on that.”

**Sorek Redmarch:**  
“He’s a ram with armor strapped on. Better than most men, aye, but not unbreakable. Still—he’s the kind of brute you want beside you when the line falters.”

**Venom:** _(quiet, deliberate)_  
“Orin gives courage. Even I stand taller when he’s near. That is no small gift.”

**Theron Sylvanranth:** _(a rare flicker of warmth)_  
“He is the mountain the storm hammers, yet still he stands. Orin is crude, direct, honest — qualities I thought long gone from men. I find… I respect him.”

---

**Sorek:**  
“[[Sir Beringer Amadis|Beringer]]’s the fulcrum still. Half Amadis, half Vogel, all duty. If the Archpaladin really did answer him, that speaks volumes.”

**Braga:** _(snorts smoke)_  
“Don’t polish him too shiny. He’s Vogel blood mostly, and I’ve seen it in him — the cold, the hard judgment. If Orin weren’t bound to him, I’d never trust him an inch. But Orin is, so I roll with it.”

**Blue Sylvanranth:** _(blushing, stammering)_  
“I–I think Beringer’s good. He listens, he… he takes time, even when he’s tired. And [[Sir Bertram Moffet|Bertram]] says—well, Bertram says he’s worth following, and if Bertram—”

_She stops suddenly, fidgeting with her sleeve._

**Venom:** _(voice soft, edged)_  
“Duty cuts deeper than blood. Beringer bleeds for every oath, every voice. That weight will crush him if he is not careful.”

**Theron:** _(cold, but not unkind)_  
“Beringer carries ghosts that are not his own. I see my own youth in him—too eager to prove, too willing to bleed. Yet… I am fond of him. If he survives the breaking, he will be more than any Vogel ever was.”

---

**Braga:**  
“And Bertram? Fool’s in love again. At least it’s [[Cornelia Bordella|Cornelia]] this time, though I’d wager half the countryside’s still got his name carved somewhere. Reckless heart, that one.”

**Sorek:**  
“Moffet blood. Loyal, scandalous, romantic. He’ll die making someone swoon, mark me.”

**Blue:** _(voice going high, awkward)_  
“H-he’s not that bad! Bertram’s brave, and funny, and… he tries. He always tries. I… I think Cornelia’s lucky, is all.”

**Venom:**  
“Romantics burn bright. But they burn everyone standing near them.”

**Theron:** _(thin smile)_  
“His heart is tinder, aye. Sparks of love, sparks of rage. But in him I see the ember that keeps a house warm. I hope it does not turn wildfire.”

---

**Sorek:**  
“[[Lyosha Ohotnik|Lyosha]]—steady as bedrock. Healer’s hands, iron inside. Already pulled off miracles.”

**Braga:**  
“Aye, but he noses too close to necromancy. Trading souls like cattle. Mercy has limits.”

**Blue:**  
“He… he fixed Bertram’s eyes. And [[Ponzio Bordella|Ponzio]]’s leg. That’s… that’s something no one else could do.”

**Venom:**  
“Mercy is a blade. Held right, it saves. Held wrong, it cuts its master.”

**Theron:**  
“Lyosha’s strength is restraint. His danger is when restraint fails. I have seen better men undone by less.”

---

**Braga:**  
“[[Thranduil]]’s a bastard, but ours. Fearsome as they come, I’ll give him that.”

**Sorek:**  
“Blunt. Terrifying. But true. He says what he is, no veil, no mask. That counts for something.”

**Blue:** _(softly)_  
“He frightens me… but I—I trust him. He never lies.”

**Venom:**  
“Fear is not mistrust. He is what he claims to be, nothing less.”

**Theron:** _(genuine fondness)_  
“Thranduil is a blade honed by grief and rage. I do not pity him; I admire him. He is the one among you who has embraced the truth of his blood without shame.”

---

**Sorek:**  
“[[Norwin]]’s clever. Clever enough to trip on it. Still, he’s got Blue slinging spells, even if half don’t spark.”

**Braga:**  
“Walking library with legs too short to run. Useful, though.”

**Blue:** _(earnestly)_  
“He—he believes in me. No one else did, not really. Even when I fizzle, he says I’m learning.”

**Venom:**  
“…Guard that belief. It’s rarer than gold.”

**Theron:**  
“Norwin hoards truths like coin. Someday he’ll spend one truth too many. But until then—he is a lantern in a darkened street.”

---

**Braga:**  
“[[Guaco]]’s a snake. Ours, but still a snake. I’d drink with him before I’d stomach [[Zara Vallytrex|Zara]].”

**Sorek:**  
“Cruel and jolly both. Knows which way the wind blows. That alone keeps him breathing.”

**Blue:**  
“He makes me laugh… and he’s sweet. That matters.”

**Venom:**  
“Sweetness hides knives. Never forget it.”

**Theron:**  
“He laughs while counting coin on burning streets. But laughter, too, has its uses. I do not underestimate him.”

---

**Sorek:**  
“[[Rykeena]]’s sharp as a stiletto. Half-orc and proud of it. She’ll rise or vanish; no in-between.”

**Braga:**  
“Ambition’s a fire. It eats as fast as it warms. She needs a cause bigger than herself.”

**Blue:**  
“I like her. She doesn’t… doesn’t hide what she is.”

**Venom:**  
“…Pride is a slow wound. She bleeds from it already.”

**Theron:**  
“Yes. Pride both shields and shackles. I wonder which she will choose when it matters.”

---

**Braga:**  
“And [[Guy]]? Mad as a bag of cats. Toys that work until they don’t.”

**Sorek:**  
“Mask that made [[Spindle]] look like [[Thalrik]]. That one’s still echoing. Wonders and wrecks, hand in hand.”

**Blue:** _(half-smiling)_  
“He talks to his pack like it’s alive. Maybe it is.”

**Venom:**  
“Madness and genius—twin blades in one sheath.”

**Theron:**  
“Madmen change the world more than sages. Guy will give both salvation and disaster, likely on the same day.”

---

**Sorek:**  
“And [[Thalrik]]—slippery dwarf. Conman with charm enough to whip mobs. Dangerous in ways even he doesn’t plan.”

**Braga:**  
“Aye, he riled [[Bard's Gate]] into a frenzy and chased the [[Galibano Family|Galibanos]] off. That’s no small feat. But mobs turn quick, and so do dwarves with debts.”

**Blue:** _(shy grin)_  
“He’s funny… and kind, sometimes. Even when he’s lying, he makes it sound like a story.”

**Venom:**  
“Lies are weapons. He wields them well. Too well.”

**Theron:** _(quiet, almost approving)_  
“Thalrik plays the fool, but I have watched his eyes. He counts every coin, every step. He may yet prove more dangerous than he knows.”

---

### Closing

**Sorek:**  
“So that’s them. A crooked lot, but kingdoms have risen with less.”

**Braga:**  
“If they don’t hold, Vogel wins. That, I’ll never swallow. But don’t ask me to trust Vogel’s get.”

**Blue:** _(hopeful, stammering)_  
“They’ll… they’ll hold. I—I know it. They have to.”

**Venom:**  
“Hope is a fragile shield. But sometimes, it is enough.”

**Theron:** _(soft, distant)_  
“They are seeds I did not expect to grow. And yet… I am fond of them. Orin, Beringer, Thranduil. Even the cracks in them shine. I would see what harvest comes.”
