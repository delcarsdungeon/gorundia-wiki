> [!important] Apocrypha
> This passage appears in scattered temple commentaries and devotional notes.  
> Some claim it represents a **vision of the Triad themselves** speaking beyond time.  
> Others insist it is merely theological allegory written by an unknown priest.

> *Copied from the marginal meditation notes of Brother Almeric of the Dawn Cathedral, who claimed the words came to him during a solar vigil. Whether this was true revelation, dream, or hopeful imagination is unknown.*

---

## The Triad Speaks

*A silver-lit hall outside time.*

*Three thrones of light stand in silent harmony. Their voices carry like bells — distinct, yet resonant.*

---

**[Pelor]:**

“The children walk in shadows too deep for their years. Yet I see mercy alive in them still.  
[[Blue Sylvanranth]]’s heart, [[Orin Kharne]]’s strength, [[Lyosha Ohotnik]]’s steady hand — they kindle light in the dark. If they endure, there is hope.”

---

**[Heironeous]:**

“Hope alone does not win wars. Steel does. [[Orin Kharne]] is a hammer worth wielding.  
[[Beringer]] carries my charge — but he falters under ghosts. He must learn: honor is not weight, but weapon.”

---

**[Pholtus]:**

“Honor is nothing without truth. [[Beringer]] bears [[House Vogel]]’s blood.  
He must face it — not bury it. Until he names it, the law of his life is incomplete.”

---

**[Pelor]:**

“[[Cornelia Bordella]]’s defiance, [[Bertram Moffet]]’s devotion — flawed, but born of love.  
Even [[Guaco Moe Lee]], sly as he is, laughs like one who still remembers the hearth. Love is worth saving.”

---

**[Heironeous]:**

“Love is not a shield. Bertram’s heart will make him reckless.  
Guaco’s cunning will turn blade in the wrong hand. They must temper loyalty with discipline, or they will fall.”

---

**[Pholtus]:**

“Discipline without law is vanity. Cornelia plays with masks.  
[[Johydee]]’s jest is rebellion, not order. If her games unravel the city further, justice will demand her accounting.”

---

**[Pelor]:**

“The villains crouch like wolves —  
[[Zara Vallytrex]], grieving and poisoned.  
[[Marco Galibano]], counting coin where lives should weigh heavier.  
[[Kursk]], the wild hunger that never softens.  
[[Wilhelm Vogel]]…”

*His voice lowers, pained.*

“…Vogel drinks the lives of innocents and calls it destiny. His road leads to shadow.”

---

**[Heironeous]:**

“Vogel is a traitor to every oath. He will stand before my blade when the time comes.  
But he is not fool — he has devils and steel alike. If the knights falter, he will take the throne.”

---

**[Pholtus]:**

“He will not take it. The law of blood condemns him.  
His arrogance blinds him to truth: every pact is written, and every written word leaves trail.  
Even devils cannot hide forever. His contracts will betray him.”

---

**[Pelor]:**

“The [[Halls of the Dead]] wait.  
[[Elizaveta]] chained, Orin’s family bound, [[Venom]] herself weighing sacrifice.  
No victory there will be clean. They will return scarred.”

---

**[Heironeous]:**

“Scars are no shame.  
[[Beringer]] prays to march there as knight, not thief. If he forgets duty, I will deny him.  
If he walks with honor, I will strengthen him.”

---

**[Pholtus]:**

“The Halls judge all. They will strip away lies. Even the gods cannot bend that law.  
If the mortals cheat, they will pay the price.”

---

**[Pelor]:**

“So fragile, these souls. Yet they carry more light than they know. I will not abandon them.”

---

**[Heironeous]:**

“Nor I. The kingdom bleeds, but steel and faith may yet bind its wounds.”

---

**[Pholtus]:**

“Then let them be weighed. Let mercy shine, let valor strike, let truth endure.  
If they are worthy, the crown will not fall. If they are not, it is no crown worth saving.”
