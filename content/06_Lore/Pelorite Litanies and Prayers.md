> [!important] PLAYER AID — DOCTRINES OF THE UN SHADOWED DAWN
> _Common teachings, prayers, and rites of the faithful of Pelor._

---

## The Three Tenets of the Dawn

### I. Illumination Before Judgment

*Let the Light reveal truth before the hand delivers justice.*

- Seek clarity before condemnation.  
- Investigate before you strike.

---

### II. Mercy Before Wrath

*The Dawn rises for all, even those who have walked in shadow.*

- Offer repentance first.  
- Wrath is last, not first.

---

### III. Stand Against the Devouring Dark

*When shadow consumes and corruption spreads, the Light does not retreat.*

Undeath. Abyss. Willful evil.

When mercy fails — act decisively.

---

## Litany of Unshadowed Flame  
_Temple Invocation_

Radiant Dawn,  
Flame that rises without fail,  
Drive back the lingering night within us.

Let Your light reveal what we would hide,  
Let Your warmth soften what we would harden,  
Let Your truth burn away deceit.

Where there is rot, bring cleansing.  
Where there is despair, bring morning.  
Where there is corruption, bring fire unyielding.

May we be vessels of Your rising.  
May we carry light into forgotten places.  
May we stand unshadowed before You.

For the night is long,  
But the Dawn is certain.

In Your light we walk.  
In Your warmth we endure.  
In Your fire we are made clean.

---

## Prayer of the Unshadowed Flame  
_Battlefield Invocation_

Radiant Dawn, see me.  
Light what I face.

If mercy may stand, let it stand.  
If fire must fall, let it fall clean.

Burn corruption.  
Guard the innocent.

Let me walk unshadowed.  
Let me rise if I fall.

Dawn is certain.

## The Last Warmth  
_Rite for the Dying_

Radiant Dawn,  
The night closes,  
But You are not far.

This soul has walked long.  
If they stumble now,  
Lift them gently.

If they fear the dark,  
Be the first light they see.

If they carry regret,  
Burn only the weight,  
Not the memory.

Let warmth find them.  
Let morning meet them.

The Dawn is certain.  
They are not alone.

## The Radiant Inquiry  
_Field Catechism of the Unshadowed Flame_  
_Authorized for use by the Third Radiant Inquiry_

### I. On Light

**Question:** What is the purpose of the Light?

**Correct Response:**  
To reveal truth, expose corruption, and guide the faithful without deceit.

---

### II. On Mercy

**Question:** When is mercy given?

**Correct Response:**  
When repentance is possible and corruption has not fully taken root.

---

### III. On Wrath

**Question:** When is wrath justified?

**Correct Response:**  
When mercy has been refused and the innocent remain threatened.

---

### IV. On Undeath

**Question:** What is owed to the undead?

**Correct Response:**  
Release. Without hesitation. Without cruelty.

---

### V. On Authority

**Question:** Who may speak in Pelor’s name?

**Correct Response:**  
Those ordained by the Church, and lay faithful who claim no authority beyond service.

---

### VI. On False Flame

**Question:** What is the gravest sin of a preacher?

**Correct Response:**  
To use the Dawn for power, coin, or personal elevation.

---

### VII. Final Measure

**Question:** What remains when doctrine fails memory?

**Correct Response:**  
Faith expressed in mercy and truth.

---

## Radiant Inquiry  
_Hardline Field Catechism_  
_Restricted Circulation – For Use During Active Investigation_

### I. On Truth

**Question:** What is done when truth is resisted?

**Required Response:**  
Truth is pressed until it stands uncovered.

---

### II. On Corruption

**Question:** Can corruption be bargained with?

**Required Response:**  
No. It must be cleansed or destroyed.

---

### III. On Mercy

**Question:** When does mercy end?

**Required Response:**  
When continued mercy endangers the innocent.

---

### IV. On Power

**Question:** What is the sign of false flame?

**Required Response:**  
Power sought for self under the guise of righteousness.

---

### V. On the Abyss

**Question:** May darkness be wielded for a righteous end?

**Required Response:**  
No. The Light requires no shadow to burn.

---

### VI. On Doubt

**Question:** What is done when doubt remains?

**Required Response:**  
Further inquiry. Until doubt is removed.

---

### VII. On Sentence

**Question:** Who carries the burden of judgment?

**Required Response:**  
The faithful who stand unshadowed.

---

### VIII. Final Proof

**Question:** What does the Dawn consume?

**Required Response:**  
All that refuses the Light.

## Prayer of Severed Shadow  
_Rite Spoken When Destroying the Undead_

Radiant Dawn, witness.

This soul is bound where it should not be.  
This flesh walks without breath.

If any spark remains,  
Receive it.

If only corruption remains,  
Burn it clean.

By light unshadowed,  
By fire unyielding,  
I sever what defies the morning.

Dawn is certain.

---

## The Writ of Rejection  
_Rite for Casting Devils Back to the Hells_

Radiant Dawn, bear witness.

This creature stands not of this soil,  
Bound by bargain, sustained by deceit.

It claims right through pact and fear.  
It claims ground not granted.

By the authority of the Light,  
By the sanctity of mortal will,  
I deny its hold.

Return to the pit that forged you.  
Return to the chain that binds you.

Your contract ends here.

Dawn is sovereign.

---

## Rite of Cleaving Light  
_Pelorite Exorcism Invocation_

Radiant Dawn, stand between.

This flesh was not made for chains.  
This spirit was not made for shadow.

Name what does not belong.  
Reveal what hides within.

If the soul yet fights, strengthen it.  
If fear yet binds, warm it.

Intruder, you are seen.  
Intruder, you are known.  
Intruder, you have no claim here.

By light that cannot be swallowed,  
By fire that cannot be owned,  
I sever you from this vessel.

Return to your darkness.  
Leave what is not yours.

Dawn is certain.
