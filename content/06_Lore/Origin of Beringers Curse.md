# Testimony: Dame Cwenheld of Marsten

**Record:** Scribed testimony retrieved by [[Sophia Amadis]]

> [!quote] Sworn Testimony — Dame Cwenheld of Marsten  
> “I had the vision two nights before it happened.  
> Not a dream—visions are not dreams.  
> This was a command. A warning. A gift from the Light.”

> [!quote]  
> “I was still on temple grounds, tending to my gear. Midday.  
> The sun dimmed, just a little.

> She was dying.”

> [!quote]  
> “I told the bishop. I told the others.  
> They weren’t ready.  
> I was.”

> [!quote]  
> “I saddled my mare. No ceremony. No fanfare.  
> Just a promise made with every hoofbeat.”

> [!quote]  
> “She ran like fire across two counties. Never broke stride.  
> Her heart burst at the crest above the villa, and I praised her with a whisper as I dismounted.  
> Then I ran.”
