# Guaco Moe Lee

Guaco Moe Lee is a halfling merchant of boundless enthusiasm, questionable restraint, and a family tree that has caused more than one clerk to quietly close their ledger and pour a drink.

Known for his talent in trade, negotiation, and improbable schemes, Guaco has accumulated both wealth and family at an impressive rate.

Despite appearances, his domestic arrangements are entirely **legal**, though the naming traditions within the Mo Lee household have baffled historians and scribes alike.

---

## The Children of Guaco Moe Lee

Guaco proudly claims the following offspring:

- **Ravio Lee**  
- **Strombo Lee**  
- **Brocco Lee**  
- **Aio Lee**  
- **Canno Lee**  
- **Chantil Lee**  
- **Patcha Coo Lee**  
- **Fagio Lee**  
- **Guaco Mo Lee Jr.**

The origins of these names are a matter of considerable speculation. When questioned, Guaco has simply stated:

> “Family tradition.”

Scholars remain unconvinced.

---

## The Wives of Guaco Moe Lee

Contrary to persistent tavern gossip, Guaco is **legally married** to the mothers of his children.

His wives include:

- **Martha**  
- **Rachael**  
- **Julia**  
- **Jemima**  
- **Paula**  
- **Betty**

Those familiar with halfling culture note that extended family households are not uncommon, though Guaco’s particular arrangement has attracted unusual attention.

Observers have also noticed a curious pattern among the names, though Guaco refuses to comment further on the matter.

---

## Reputation

Guaco’s companions have learned to expect three things whenever he introduces a member of his family:

1. The name will raise eyebrows.  
2. The explanation will not clarify matters.  
3. Guaco will somehow convince everyone it makes perfect sense.

Whether this reflects a shrewd merchant’s humor or simply the chaotic joy of halfling family life remains an open question.
