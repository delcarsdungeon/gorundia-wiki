# The Archon of Erythnul

The **Archon of Erythnul** was a shapeshifting priest-warrior devoted to [[Erythnul the Many]], the god of slaughter, panic, and riotous bloodshed.

Operating in and around **[[Bard's Gate]]**, the Archon served as a hidden agent of chaos, loosely aligned with a **Yuan-Ti serpent cult** that had infiltrated portions of the city's criminal underworld.

His true name was never discovered.

---

# Methods

The Archon specialized in **deception and engineered chaos** rather than straightforward violence.

Known abilities included:

- shapeshifting or magical disguise  
- illusion magic  
- infiltration of cult networks  
- provoking riots and panic among civilian populations  

Rather than attacking directly, the Archon preferred to manipulate events so that enemies destroyed one another.

A favored tactic was to **impersonate prominent individuals**, allowing suspicion and violence to spread through the city.

---

# The Bloody-Handed Deception

The Archon’s most infamous act was the murder of **[[Elyndra Sylvanranth]]**, carried out while disguised as **[[Orin Kharne]]**.

The killing triggered widespread confusion and briefly convinced many that Orin himself had become a murderous outlaw.

The deception earned Orin the unwanted title **“Bloody-Handed.”**

These events are recorded as **[[The Bloody-Handed Deception]]**.

---

# The Market Riot

During his flight from the pursuing adventurers later known as the **Iron Wolves**, the Archon unleashed chaos in a crowded marketplace.

Using illusion magic, he created **multiple false copies of Orin Kharne** which began attacking civilians.

The resulting panic drew the attention of the **City Watch** and several **paladins of Heironeous**, who mistakenly believed the adventurers themselves were responsible for the violence.

The confusion led to a disastrous confrontation in the streets.

During this incident the wizard **Fiona** was slain by a paladin while attempting to ward off pursuit.

The Archon used the chaos to escape.

---

# Death

Despite his talent for deception, the Archon’s career as a servant of Erythnul proved remarkably short.

Within a single session of direct conflict with the adventuring party, he was cornered and killed.

His death ended his immediate schemes in Bard's Gate, though the cult networks he had cultivated continued to cause trouble long afterward.

---

# Legacy

Although the Archon himself did not survive long, his actions had lasting effects:

- The murder of Elyndra Sylvanranth destabilized political factions in Bard's Gate.
- [[Orin Kharne]] was saddled with the enduring nickname **“Bloody-Handed.”**
- The incident revealed the presence of **Yuan-Ti cult activity** in the region.

To historians of the city, the Archon remains an example of how **a single agent of chaos can ignite conflicts far larger than himself**.
