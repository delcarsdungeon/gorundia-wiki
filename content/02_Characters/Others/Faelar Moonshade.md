# Lord Faelar Moonshade

> “History is written by the victors, but it is the scholar who uncovers the truth beneath their words.”  
> — [[Sorek Redmarch]]

## Basics

Born: Unknown (High Elven lineage)  
Race: High Elf  
Occupation: Historian, scholar, curator of ancient lore  
Affiliation: Vassal-scholar attached to [[Duchy of Araisal]]  
Patrons: [[Eldric Allaric]], [[Maris Allaric]]  
Family Connection: Distant relative of [[Nienor Allarson]]  

Lord Faelar Moonshade is one of the most respected historians in Gorundia. Though he holds minor noble status, his true power lies not in land or soldiers but in **knowledge of the ancient world**.

Known as the **Keeper of the Eldritch Scrolls**, Faelar maintains and interprets a number of rare and dangerous historical texts. His expertise in dead languages, arcane history, and ancient civilizations makes him a frequent advisor to noble houses and scholars alike.

---
## Background

Faelar was born into an old elven lineage tied to a long-ruined city whose libraries once rivaled the greatest archives of the ancient world.

From a young age he was educated by master scholars in:

- ancient history  
- linguistics  
- rune lore  
- pre-cataclysmic civilizations  

His lifelong work has focused on the forgotten cultures that shaped the Flanaess—particularly the **Suel Imperium** and the **Baklunish Empire**.

Over centuries of study he has recovered, translated, and preserved numerous texts that would otherwise have vanished from history.

Though technically independent, Faelar works under the patronage of **[[Eldric Allaric]]** and **[[Maris Allaric]]**, whose court in the [[Duchy of Araisal]] maintains one of the greatest scholarly archives in the realm.

## Appearance

![[Faelar Moonshade.png|360]]

Faelar embodies the refined austerity typical of elder elven scholars.

- Tall and slender  
- Long silver hair, sometimes tied into a loose braid  
- Sharp grey eyes with an intense, analytical gaze  
- Pale, severe features  

He typically wears layered robes of **deep blue and silver**, embroidered with faint arcane glyphs.

Rather than weapons, he carries:

- scroll tubes  
- quills and parchment  
- translation tools and rune guides  

To Faelar, **knowledge itself is the only weapon worth wielding.**

---
## Role in the Duchy of Araisal

Faelar serves as the **chief curator of the Allaric Archives**, a massive collection of ancient manuscripts, magical texts, and historical records.

His responsibilities include:

- cataloging dangerous or rare magical texts  
- translating ancient languages  
- advising on historical precedents and treaties  
- assisting noble houses with research into artifacts and ruins  

While not formally part of court politics, his findings often influence **strategic decisions made by House Allaric**.

Faelar also maintains a distant familial connection to **[[Nienor Allarson]]**, Countess Velnyar. This relationship occasionally grants him access to political circles beyond Araisal when historical questions intersect with diplomacy.

---
## Personality

Faelar is intensely intellectual and introspective.

Traits commonly attributed to him:

- Analytical  
- Reserved  
- Patient  
- Dryly humorous when engaged in scholarly debate  

He has little tolerance for courtly frivolity and prefers the quiet company of manuscripts to the chaos of noble gatherings.

Those who earn his respect find him to be an **engaging and formidable conversationalist**, capable of recalling centuries of historical context in astonishing detail.

---
## Skills & Expertise

### Ancient Languages
Faelar is capable of reading and translating numerous dead tongues including:

- Suel dialects  
- Baklunish script traditions  
- archaic elven runic forms

### Arcane Lore
Though not a practicing mage, he possesses extensive theoretical knowledge of magical traditions and artifacts.

### Archaeological Research
Faelar often organizes scholarly expeditions to ancient ruins in search of lost knowledge.

### Historical Diplomacy
His knowledge of forgotten treaties, dynastic conflicts, and ancient alliances has quietly resolved more than one political crisis.

---
## Notable Achievements

### Recovery of the Windrider Codex
Faelar recovered and translated the **Windrider Codex**, an ancient elven manuscript describing aerial magic and early flight enchantments.

### Advisor to the Council of Elders
He was called upon to interpret the ancient history of the **Twin Elvish Kingdoms**, helping guide diplomatic relations among the elves of Gorundia.

### Curator of the Allaric Archives
Faelar’s most enduring work is the preservation and expansion of the **Allaric Archives**, now considered one of the most important scholarly repositories in the realm.

- A dangerous magical manuscript has gone missing from the archives.
- Faelar’s research uncovers evidence that a **current political claim rests on a falsified historical record**.
