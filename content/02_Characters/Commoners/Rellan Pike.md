# Rellan Pike

**Official Title:** Examiner of Petitions to the Crown  
**Occupation:** Royal security examiner, former thief  
**Age:** Mid-40s  
**Status:** Quiet servant of the Crown

---

## Overview

Rellan Pike is a discreet operative in royal service whose official role is **Examiner of Petitions to the Crown**. In practice, he quietly screens letters, gifts, and packages sent to important officials of the realm.

Anything dangerous rarely reaches its intended recipient.

Formerly a talented burglar, Pike was caught years ago and offered a choice between prison and service. He chose service and has proven exceptionally useful ever since.

---

## Reputation

Among courtiers he is viewed as a quiet bureaucrat who deals with correspondence.

Among those who know better, he is something else entirely.

In the underworld his name carries quiet respect. He knows the reputation of [[Guaco Moe Lee]], though the two have never formally met.

Professionals recognize professionals.

---

## Skills & Talents

- Gift and letter screening  
- Lockpicking and trap detection  
- Detecting coded messages  
- Quiet investigation and surveillance  
- Discreet problem removal

---

## Current Duties

Recently assigned to assist with the overwhelming flood of letters and gifts arriving for [[Beringer]] after the arena incident.

Most of his work involves identifying:

- concealed blades  
- poison vials  
- blackmail attempts  
- coded political messages

Occasionally he also finds love letters.

These he leaves for the Lord Marshal.

---

## Quote

*"Mostly I make sure nobody hides a knife inside a bouquet."*
