# Naira Sylvanranth

**Lord Naira Sylvanranth** was born to the powerful but complicated House Sylvanranth.

Handsome, clever, and naturally gifted with both blade and spell, Naira once possessed every advantage a noble heir could hope for.

Unfortunately, he also possessed every opportunity to squander them.

Now disowned and drifting through the courts of the capital, Naira lives on reputation, charm, and borrowed coin—an elegant wreck of a nobleman whose fall is as dramatic as his promise once was.

> “Some sons inherit their father's sword. Naira inherited the sheath, left the blade behind, and tried to pawn the scabbard for dice money.”  
> — [[Sir Sorek Redmarch]]

---

# Appearance

Naira is tall and elegant, with the striking features common to those of mixed elven and human blood. His short blond hair is usually worn in a deliberately careless style, and his clothes are always just a little too fine for a man with no fortune.

His clothing remains expensive—often far more expensive than his current financial situation justifies.

Even in ruin, he presents himself as though the world still belongs to him.

And in some ways, he still believes it does.

# Heritage

Father: [[Theron Sylvanranth]]  
Mother: [[Cassara Ravenshade]]  
Siblings: [[Elyndra Sylvanranth]], [[Dana Freeblade]] (half-sister), [[Blue Sylvanranth]] (half-sister)

Birth Year: circa **1185**  
Race: **Half-Elf**

Naira and Elyndra were the children of Theron's marriage to **Cassara Ravenshade**, a minor noblewoman of scholarly reputation.

Much of their upbringing fell under Cassara's guidance rather than their father's distant hand. She provided the steadiness and restraint that the Sylvanranth household otherwise lacked.

Cassara died in **1210**, when Naira was still a young man. Her death removed the one moderating influence in the family, and many who knew the household later remarked that Naira's slow unraveling began not long afterward.

---

# The Lost Heir

In his youth, Naira appeared destined for greatness.

He showed real talent in:

- **Swordsmanship**, particularly formal dueling  
- **Arcane magic**, especially illusion and enchantment  
- **Courtly diplomacy and charm**

Unfortunately, these gifts were paired with a profound lack of discipline.

Where his sister [[Elyndra Sylvanranth]] matured into grace and restraint, Naira embraced indulgence, gambling, and reckless social entanglements.

Theron, famously distant as a father, allowed the problem to grow unchecked.

---

# The Fall

Recent events finally forced a reckoning.

Theron Sylvanranth formally **cut Naira off from the family fortune**, stripping him of inheritance and influence.

Instead, Theron named **[[Blue Sylvanranth]]** as heir to the house.

The decision shocked many within the court.

Though **[[Dana Freeblade]]**, the elder half-sister, had once renounced any claim to the Sylvanranth legacy, recent events have quietly restored her standing within the family hierarchy.

For the first time in his life, Naira found himself **without the safety net of his father's name and wealth**.

---

# The Bannerhall Incident

Matters worsened dramatically during the recent **Bannerhall gathering**.

Already strained relations between Naira and [[Dana Freeblade]] erupted into open confrontation.

Dana proved unimpressed with his behavior.

The argument escalated until Naira was physically thrown into the mud and publicly humiliated—effectively given the treatment reserved for an unruly squire rather than a noble heir.

The incident became instant gossip throughout the court.

---

# Current Circumstances

Naira now drifts through the capital in an increasingly precarious state.

His situation includes:

- **Heavy gambling debts**
- The sale of his personal sword to cover expenses
- Temporary lodging with various acquaintances
- Growing scandal attached to his name

In short, he has become the classic medieval aristocratic disaster:

A trust-fund noble who assumed the money would never run out.
