## Overview

The Great Lady Aerilaya Qinsandoral, thought to be the last surviving member of the High Elf noble family, is called queen in exile by her supporters, a title she rejects, but she takes the burden of leadership for the elvish diospora. She is rumored to be arriving to lead the expedition far west to see the status of the High Elf cities, meaning Bard's Gate will be an inevitable stopover.
