# Braga “the Brazen”

_Marshal of the West • Knight of Heironeous • Wife of Baron Orin Kharne_

> “Dame Braga, [[Warden of the West]]—she’s wearing a title that weighs heavier than her armor, and I’d wager she feels it every damn day. Plenty of nobles still whisper about ‘Braga the Bitch,’ like she’s still that poor knight-errant scraping by on tournament winnings and fixing peasants’ problems.
> 
> But they’re blind. She’s got the heart and strength to outmatch half the heroes in the realm—if she ever realizes it herself.
> 
> Her marriage to Orin? That’s the real thing. Two scarred people making each other stronger. Give her time. The West doesn’t know what it’s got yet.”  
> — **Sir [[Sorek Redmarch]]**

## Core Profile

**Title:** Marshal of the West  
**Known As:** Braga the Brazen, formerly _Braga the Bitch_  
**House:** Durathen (by blood) / Kharne (by marriage)  
**Faith:** Heironeous  
**Spouse:** Baron Orin Kharne  
**Reputation:** Brutally honest knight-errant turned regional marshal  
**Status:** Rising power in the western marches

---

## Appearance

### General Build

Short and broad — **5'5"**, thick-shouldered and visibly strong.  
Built more like a **farm girl who never stopped hauling grain sacks** than a courtly knight.

To the inattentive eye she may look **chunky or soft**.  
Anyone who’s seen her fight knows better.

### Features

- Round face
    
- Button nose
    
- Sleepy blue eyes
    
- Long black hair that refuses to behave no matter how hard her ladies try
    

Her armor shows **real wear** — dents, scratches, and patched straps from years on the road.

---

## Personality

Braga is many things. Pleasant is rarely one of them.

She is blunt, stubborn, and fiercely honest — the kind of knight who would rather break a jaw than flatter a noble.

What she **is not** is cruel.

The nickname _“Braga the Bitch”_ began as mockery from wealthier knights who disliked being outperformed by a poor knight-errant with a bad attitude.

She embraced it.

Her defining traits:
- **Unshakeable courage**
- **Zero tolerance for hypocrisy**
- **Protective of the weak**
- **Deeply uncomfortable with politics**

Braga understands court etiquette perfectly well.  
She simply **despises pretending people are better than they are.**

---

## Habits & Mannerisms

- Smokes or chews cigars when nervous
- Paces while thinking through problems    
- Often stares at the ground while concentrating    
- Laughs rarely, but when she does it is loud and genuine    
- Swears like a cattle drover    

Her voice is **low and husky**, like someone who grew up shouting across fields.

---
## Faith

Braga is sincerely devout to **Heironeous**, but in the practical soldier’s sense.

She prays before battle, keeps her word, and believes courage is a sacred duty.

Her husband Orin’s near-total lack of religious interest **quietly frustrates her**, though she rarely pushes the issue.

---
## Warhorse

**The Biscuit of Destiny**

A stubborn but loyal warhorse who has carried Braga through more tournaments and battles than she can count.

Braga claims the horse “chooses when destiny happens.”  
Most people assume this means **the horse bites someone important.**

---
## Key Relationships

### Baron Orin Kharne

**Husband**

Their courtship was anything but conventional.

During the Grand Tournament at Bard’s Gate, Orin defeated Braga in combat, disarmed her, and **kissed her in front of the crowd**.

The next day she was scheduled to face Malik Red-Hands — a knight infamous for killing opponents in tournaments.

Instead, Braga invoked the **Rite of the Unbroken Queen**.

Orin and his companions fought through the ritual trial to “rescue” her, sealing the rite with blood and battle.

Their marriage was forged in combat and mutual respect.

What binds them:

- Valor    
- Honesty    
- Shared scars    
- A mutual hatred of bullshit    

---
### Dame Venymmara Gylenda
**Blood-Sister / Mentor**

[[Venym]] was the first knight Braga ever met who proved that an outsider could succeed **without compromising their integrity**.

They met defending a small village and became inseparable afterward.

To Braga, Venym is:

- A sister    
- A mentor    
- A living proof that the system can be beaten   

Shared secret:  
_The body buried beneath the old oak in Shardhollow._

---
### Fancy Korac

**Friend (Complicated)**

[[Fancy Korac]] is one of the few people Braga treats with relaxed familiarity.

Their friendship has weathered several spectacular arguments and at least one thrown window.

---
### [[Edwyn Durathen]]

**Cousin**

Part of the extended Durathen feud and political entanglements surrounding Braga’s family.

Their relationship is complicated by family politics and lingering resentments.

## History

Braga’s early career was that of a **wandering knight-errant**.

She traveled the countryside solving problems nobody else cared about:

- Bandits    
- Monster infestations    
- Peasant disputes    
- Minor skirmishes    

She won tournaments when she needed money and lost friends when she refused to flatter nobles.

For years she was mocked as:

- **Braga the Broke**    
- **Braga the Broken**    
- **Braga the Bellicose**    
- **Braga the Bitch**    

Everything changed at the **Great Tournament at Bard’s Gate**.

Her courage and performance forced the king himself to intervene.

Before the assembled nobles, the king declared:

**“Let none call her Bitch again. She is Braga the Brazen.”**

He then named her **Marshal of the West**.

The insult became a title.

---

## Current Struggles

Braga is still adjusting to her new position.

For most of her life:

- Her opinion did not matter.    
- Nobles mocked her.    
- Power was something other people had.    

Now she commands troops and influences politics across the western frontier.

She knows the responsibility is enormous.

And she intends to prove the king right.
