# Lady Ellynna Ravelle

>[!quote] *"She’s never held a sword, but I’ve seen men fall harder from one glance than from a lance to the gut."* — [[Sorek Redmarch]]

---
## Overview

Lady **Ellynna Ravelle** holds no lands and commands no soldiers, yet few nobles dare ignore her.

A courtesan of formidable reputation and a social tactician without peer, Ellynna navigates the court like a dancer with a dagger hidden in her fan. She hosts some of the most exclusive—and dangerous—salons in the capital. Within those candlelit rooms, reputations are forged, alliances quietly arranged, and the occasional noble marriage encouraged or destroyed.

Though she bears no formal title beyond *Lady*, her influence stretches across duchies, and her name appears far more often in whispered rumors than in heraldic registries.

---
## Appearance

![[Ellynna Ravelle.png|340]]

Ellynna is undeniably beautiful—tall and lithe with **dark copper hair** worn in elaborate twists, **pale luminous skin**, and **violet-gray eyes** that seem to miss nothing.

Her voice is low, sultry, and precisely measured.

She dresses to unsettle. Her gowns are elegant and impeccable, yet always a fraction too daring to be considered entirely proper.

Her wardrobe reflects her intentions:

- **Dark silks** when she is playing dangerous games  
- **Warm tones** when hosting salons and cultivating alliances  
- **White** when she intends to embarrass someone publicly

## Personality

Ellynna is charming, calculating, and rarely sincere—unless sincerity would be the most disarming weapon in the room.

She maintains a constant air of **lazy amusement**, often appearing bored during court proceedings before delivering a perfectly timed remark that quietly destroys someone’s position.

Despite her reputation, she is rarely cruel.

Her version of mercy simply involves **allowing someone the dignity of ruining themselves**.

She is:

- fluent in gossip  
- dangerously well-read  
- a master of social timing  
- utterly comfortable weaponizing inconvenient truths  

Her greatest pleasure is not cruelty, but **control**—especially watching powerful men underestimate her.

## Romantic Entanglements

### [[Naira Sylvanranth]]

The most infamous of her rumored lovers.

Naira, estranged son of [[Theron Sylvanranth]], is a brooding stormcloud of a man. Ellynna appears to enjoy provoking the thunder.

Some say she tamed him.  
Others say she simply let him think he was dangerous.

---

### Sir Jeran Mallan

A rigidly moral knight whose reputation survived years of campaigning—until a single winter ball where he spent far too long laughing with Ellynna.

His wife has not forgiven either of them.

---

### [[Thaliel Thornquil]]

The bard insists nothing ever happened between them.

Ellynna insists **nothing ever stopped**.

---

## Relationships

### [[Velkora Thorne]]

A favorite sparring partner in conversation and innuendo.

Their exchanges are famously sharp, yet there is genuine respect beneath the rivalry.

---

### [[Wilhelm Vogel]]

Ellynna has never flirted with him.

That alone makes many people nervous.

She treats the duke like **a dangerous bear**—one that must remain fed, calm, and preferably several rooms away.

---
### [[Calira Meroven]]

Ellynna considers Calira far too kind for the capital’s politics.

She quietly keeps an eye on her.

She would never admit this aloud.

---
### [[Sorek Redmarch]]

Their relationship is built on blunt honesty and mutual tolerance.

Ellynna once described him as:

> “A grizzly with manners.”

He has never corrected her.
