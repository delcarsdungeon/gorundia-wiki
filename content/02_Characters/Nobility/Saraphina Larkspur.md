# Dame Saraphina Larkspur

>[!quote] “Dame Larkspur holds a knight’s title, a knight’s land, and the attention of half the capital.”
>_pause_
>“Only thing missing is the knight part.” - Sorek Redmarch

Dame **Saraphina Larkspur** is one of the capital’s most notorious beauties—a woman whose presence tends to rearrange conversations, marriages, and occasionally political alliances.

She is dazzling, shamelessly flirtatious, and entirely comfortable with the reputation that follows her through every court hall in Gorundia.

Most people agree on two things about Dame Saraphina:

1. She is **dangerously charming**.  
2. She **should never be left alone with anyone’s husband**.

---

## Basics

Born: Unknown (likely late 1200s OR)  
House: Larkspur (minor landed knightly estate)  
Title: Dame  
Status: Landed knight and court socialite  
Residence: A small Larkspur manor outside the capital and various court guest chambers  

---
## Appearance

Saraphina is widely regarded as one of the most beautiful women in the capital.

**Canonical features**

- Platinum-blonde hair, thick and luminous
- Pale skin and striking light eyes
- A confident, mischievous smile
- A figure that guarantees she is rarely ignored in any room

Her fashion choices are famous at court:

- Wide-brimmed feathered hats
- Velvet chokers and jeweled pendants
- Gowns cut **just shy of scandalous**

Her portraits are known for being **suggestive without ever quite crossing the line into impropriety**, a boundary she appears to navigate deliberately.

---
## Reputation

Saraphina’s reputation rests on three pillars:

**Beauty**  
Few people in the capital rival her presence in a ballroom.

**Scandal**  
Her romantic entanglements are the subject of constant gossip.

**Fearless Impropriety**  
She has an uncanny ability to test the limits of court etiquette without quite breaking them.

Several marriages have reportedly survived her attention.

A few have not.

---
## The Knight Who Never Fights

Dame Saraphina technically holds the title of **landed knight**, with a modest estate attached to the Larkspur name.

However, one detail fuels endless court gossip:

No one has ever seen her in armor.

She has never entered a tournament.

She has never ridden to war.

Veteran knights quietly note that she appears to treat swords more as **fashion accessories than battlefield tools**.

The matter is occasionally whispered about.

Never in her hearing.

---
## Known Incident: The Dragon Correspondence

Following the arena incident in which [[Beringer]] faced down a dragon, Dame Saraphina sent the Lord Marshal:

- A **silver locket containing a scandalously suggestive miniature portrait**
- A **boldly flirtatious letter**

This was particularly notable because **Beringer is already married**.

The correspondence was intercepted and processed by his household staff before reaching him.

According to [[Alard Fenric]]’s ledger notes, the letter required **careful diplomatic handling to avoid a court scandal**.

---

## Personality

Saraphina enjoys three things above all:

- attention  
- bold gestures  
- watching serious men become uncomfortable  

She is clever enough to know exactly how far she can push propriety without suffering consequences.

Whether this makes her reckless or brilliant depends entirely on whom you ask.

---
## Rumors

- She once caused a duel between two barons during a winter court ball.
- Several noble houses quietly forbid their heirs from courting her.
- She owns a suit of armor somewhere on the Larkspur estate. No one has ever seen it worn.
- Some believe she is far more politically perceptive than she pretends to be.
