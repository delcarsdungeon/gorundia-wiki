## House Valcorin of Lowbridge

A modest but old **river-holding family**, controlling ferry rights and stone bridges along a mid-kingdom trade artery.

**Arms:** Azure, a silver heron upon a stone arch  
**Reputation:** Respectable, solvent, politically cautious  
**Known for:** Falconry, orchard wines, advantageous but quiet marriages

The Valcorins built their fortune on **tolls and patience**.

They survive by **never being the tallest tower in the storm.**

---

## Public Persona

Alessandra does not reside in the city.

She remains at the **Bordella estates**, overseeing:

- Tenantry  
- Household management  
- Her sons’ education  
- Seasonal obligations  

She appears in civic settings **only when necessary**.

She does not involve herself in municipal politics.  
She does not advise publicly.  
She does not contradict her husband.

---

## Private Reality

Alessandra understands her marriage precisely.

It was **strategic.**  
It is **functional.**  
It is **not affectionate.**

Ponzio hunts.  
Ponzio governs.  
Ponzio cultivates alliances elsewhere.

Alessandra maintains **continuity**.

When he confuses the boys’ names, she corrects him gently:

> “Rinaldo, my lord.”

Never sharp.  
Never embarrassed.  
Never defensive.

The servants notice.

The boys notice.

---

## Amusements & Interests

### Patron of Light Theatre

Her true indulgence.

At the estates she sponsors:

- Traveling troupes  
- Masked recitations  
- Tragic romances  
- Seasonal hall performances  

She favors stories of:

- Loyalty tested  
- Betrayal endured  
- Love surviving humiliation  

Scripts are delivered to her personally.

She annotates them.

She enjoys **drama — safely contained within a stage.**

---

## Conservatory & Perfumed Gardens

A carefully tended greenhouse and herb garden containing:

- Calming tinctures  
- Imported resins  
- Rare flowering vines  

Not poison.  
Not scandal.

Just **refinement**.

Control in small things.

# Children of House Bordella

## Household Dynamics

Ponzio visits **irregularly**.

When present:

- He speaks of legacy, not bedtime  
- He addresses tutors more than sons  
- He praises “strength” in abstract terms  

Alessandra ensures:

- Lessons continue  
- Accounts remain precise  
- Tenants remain loyal  
- Stability never cracks  

She does not challenge him.

She **outlasts him**.
