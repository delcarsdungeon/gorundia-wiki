# Marco Galibano

## Title
**Patriarch of the Galibano Family**

Marco Galibano is the current head of the **Galibano merchant syndicate**, a sprawling family enterprise that dominates construction, quarrying, and stonework contracts throughout [[Gorundia]] and especially its home territory of the Duchy of [[House Vogel|Grevandar]].

Within the family he is still affectionately called **“Little Marco”**, a nickname inherited from his grandfather, the original Marco Galibano.

Outsiders who attempt the name usually do so **exactly once.**

---

## Appearance

Marco is a **broad-shouldered older man**, thick through the chest and forearms in a way that betrays a lifetime of real labor before wealth softened his hands.

His hair has gone mostly iron-grey, worn short and disciplined, with a trimmed beard that shows more silver each year.

Common traits:

- tailored merchant noble clothing
- heavy signet ring of the Galibano family
- thick callused hands despite wealth
- deliberate, measured movements
- sharp dark eyes that constantly assess a room

He still occasionally works **hammer and chisel**, carving stone in private workshops.

The dwarves approve.

They distrust men whose hands have grown soft.

---

## Background

The Galibano family rose from **stonecutters and builders**, immigrants who made their fortune supplying masonry to Bard’s Gate during its expansion generations ago.

Marco inherited not just wealth, but **a network of contracts, guild influence, and quiet coercion**.

Under his leadership the family expanded beyond honest stonework into:

- construction monopolies
- logistics
- debt leverage
- discreet criminal enforcement

Despite this expansion, Marco insists the family remain **builders first**.

He still walks quarry sites and inspects new projects personally.

Workers say he can tell **bad stone by the sound of the hammer alone.**

---

## Temperament

Marco Galibano is known for being **polished, articulate, and impeccably polite** in public dealings.

Traits often noted:

- urbane and controlled
- ruthlessly practical
- loyal to family above all
- capable of sudden, frightening anger

Most negotiations with Marco feel like **pleasant business meetings**.

Until they stop being pleasant.

---

## Beliefs / Ideology

Marco believes in **structure**.

Cities, families, and power all require the same thing:

> strong foundations and disciplined maintenance.

He despises chaos, amateur criminals, and reckless violence that threatens the stability of Bard’s Gate.

In his view, **the Galibanos are caretakers of the city’s bones.**

## Reputation

Among merchants and guildmasters of [[Bard's Gate]], Marco Galibano is known for:

- always honoring contracts
- controlling most major construction projects
- maintaining quiet influence with the city’s dwarven craftsmen
- responding brutally when the family is crossed

Among dwarves he is sometimes referred to as:

**“Marco Galibano, son of [[Ruggerio Galibano]], son of Marco Galibano.”**

The full lineage is spoken with the solemnity of a clan introduction.

---

## Limitations

- advancing age
- relies heavily on family lieutenants
- prefers indirect control to personal action

Marco no longer handles violence himself.

But the **family still remembers how he used to.**
