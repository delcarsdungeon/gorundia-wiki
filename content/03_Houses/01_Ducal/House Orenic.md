# Duchy of Royonad
### House Orenic

**Ruling House:** House Orenic  
**Seat:** Royograd  
**Symbol:** The Storm Lynx  
**Reputation:** Fierce, Loyal, Unapologetic

> [!quote] Sorek Redmarch
> “Orenics don’t whisper about power.  
> They stand where it can see them.”

---

## Current Political Climate

Royonad remains openly loyal to the **Crown** and publicly supportive of the **Lord Marshal’s appointment**.

However, recent developments have made the duchy’s visibility in national politics impossible to ignore.

---

## The Marriage Alliance

**Alistair Amadis the Younger**, heir to **House Amadis of Blackbriar**, is formally betrothed to **Lady Milena Orenic**, niece of **Duke Vladek Orenic**.

This is **not a minor match**.

It binds:

- The heir of **Blackbriar**
- The ruling house of **Royonad**
- And, indirectly, the **office of the Lord Marshal**

In Royonad, the sentiment is **celebratory**.

Elsewhere, observers note the **consolidation of visible loyalties**.

---
## The Druzhnik

Sir **Beringer Amadis’** most visible bodyguard is an **Orenic druzhnik** — a warrior of Royonad’s traditional retinue class.

To Orenic eyes, this is **honor**.

To others, it is **proximity**.

The image of **Orenic steel** standing beside the **Lord Marshal**, while the Amadis heir marries into **House Orenic**, has fueled considerable discussion.

---
## Duke Vladek Orenic

Duke Vladek has **never been subtle**.

He has publicly stated that scrutiny of **Duke Wilhelm Vogel** was:

> “Long overdue.”

He has also been clear that **any final judgment rests with the King**.

Despite his outspoken nature, Vladek has demonstrated restraint:

- No troop mobilization beyond standard readiness  
- No formal denunciation beyond his initial remarks  
- No attempt to leverage the situation for territorial advantage  

His position may be summarized as:

> “It was time.  
> But it is not my crown.”

---
## Military Posture

Royograd remains **fully garrisoned**.

- Cavalry drills continue at **winter strength**  
- Border vigilance remains focused **eastward**  
- No abnormal mobilization has been observed  

---
## Whispers Across the Realm

Some nobles speculate about an emerging **Amadis–Orenic axis**.

Others note that **Duke Vladek’s rhetoric aligns closely with the Lord Marshal’s recent actions**.

A few suggest Royonad now stands **closer to the center of power than at any point since the Second War of Succession**.

Royonad rejects accusations of ambition.

It frames its position as **loyalty — loudly stated and plainly demonstrated**.

---
## Player-Facing Summary

Royonad stands **proud and visible**.

The marriage of **Amadis the Younger** to **Milena Orenic** strengthens bonds between duchies and ties Royonad closely to the **Lord Marshal’s orbit**.

Duke Vladek has made his view of **Grevandar** clear — but has shown restraint pending the **King’s ruling**.

Whether this moment represents **alignment, loyalty, or rising influence** depends on who is watching.
